# How to Use Task Manager

This guide will walk you through exactly how to use the live web application to manage your projects, track your productivity, and switch up your workspace theme.

---

## 🌐 Deployed URL

You can access the live application directly in your web browser at the following link:
👉 **[https://task-manager-workspace.vercel.app/](https://task-manager-workspace.vercel.app/)**

---

## 📌 Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Log Into Your Account

When you first open the link, you will see a clean login screen.

1. Enter your Username and Password.
2. Click the Login button.
3. The app will securely connect to the backend, verify your account, and load your personal workspace dashboard.
   [![Login/Signup](/images/login.png)]

### Step 2: Switch to Dark Theme

If you prefer working in a sleek dark interface to reduce eye strain, you can switch modes instantly.

1. Look at the bottom of sidebar.
2. Click the Theme Toggle (usually a Moon 🌙 or Sun ☀️ icon).
3. The entire application will instantly switch to a beautifully styled Dark Theme. The app automatically saves this choice, so it stays dark even if you refresh the page!
   [![dark theme](/images/theme_dark.png)
   ![light theme](/images/theme_light.png)]

### Step 3: Create and Manage Your Tasks

Your workspace makes it incredibly simple to keep track of your daily to-do lists.

- **Add a New Task:** Click the task creation button, type in a clear title, add a detailed description, set a due date, and choose its starting status (like Todo or In Progress).
- **Update a Task:** As you work on a project, you can update its status or details to show your progress. Move it along from Todo -> In Progress -> Completed.
- **Delete a Task:** If a task is no longer needed, simply click the delete icon to remove it from your workspace tracking board.
  [![Add task](/images/add_task.png)
  ![Task List](/images/task_list.png)]

### Step 4: Check Your Analytics Dashboard

Want to see how much progress you are making? Head over to the analytics tab to view your visual statistics.

1. **System Overview Metrics:** Look at the quick counter cards to see your total tasks and check your completion percentages at a single glance.
2. **Productivity Trends:** Check out the weekly and monthly graphs to see your workflow history and track how fast you are knocking out goals over time.
   [![Weekly and Monthly Trends](/images/Analytics%20Metrices.png)
   ![Dashboard Summary Stats](/images/Dashboard.png)]

### Step 5: Using the App on Your Mobile Phone

You don't need to be at a desktop computer to manage your workload.

- Open the link on your phone or resize your web browser window.
- The application is fully mobile-responsive. The desktop sidebar automatically hides away into a mobile-friendly menu button, and all your task cards scale perfectly onto small mobile screens without any overlapping text or broken layouts.
  [![Mobile ](/images/mobile.png)
  ![Mobile 2](/images/mobile1.png)]
